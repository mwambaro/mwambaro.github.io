Platforms

An ecosystem of five platforms

01

Jobperon

An Artificial Intelligence-powered job search engine that makes job hunting natural. Describe the job you want and get curated results displayed.

02

Chaperon

A social support system that uses Artificial Intelligence for job placement and adds housing, meals, allowance fees, and training. It is a social form of loaning.

03

GetClientele

An Artificial Intelligence-intensive clientele distribution platform that uses navigation technologies.

04

eCard Wireless

A cloud infrastructure that ensures connectivity and delivers a multi-purpose e-card serving as wallet, identity document, passport, driver’s license, and unified access to official and social documents.

05

AORI

AORI uses mergers and acquisitions to merge or acquire companies, individual properties, and governmental services so they form one unified conglomerate in which one job is enough to get everything one needs to live with dignity.

Philosophy

Laastras, LaaS, Homocracy

Laastras stands for Leadership as a Service Trade System. It is an application of LaaS, Leadership as a Service, operationalized by Homocracy, Governance by Equals.

Morshux

Management of Resources that Support Human Existence: a social and economic platform where one role yields Porshux.

Porshux

Portion of Resources that Support Human Existence: everything an individual needs to live with dignity.

SerX

Services Exchange: instead of using currency, it uses service as the means of exchange.

VirCu

Virtual Currency: a form of trade using Decentralized Finance to let individuals pursue creativity on their Porshux and gain extra comfort. It complements SerX.

Institutions

Four institutions mirrored by the ecosystem

HDN – Human Demography and Nutrition

Mirrored by Jobperon and Chaperon, it inventories all humans as well as all life-supporting resources.

CTP – Chain of Transformation and Production

Parallel to AORI, Acquire and Integrate, it makes sure life-supporting resources are transformed and produced into consumables.

WTSC – World Trade System Consortium

Rooted in eCard Wireless, it synchronizes all other institutions, provides legal protection, digitizes everything, and maintains the trade-centered Constitution.

IFaSS – International Fair Social System

Emerging from GetClientele, it makes sure every individual gets all they need delivered to them.
